Brief: Shraddha outlines a strategy to improve LinkedIn connection request acceptance rates by establishing familiarity before reaching out. Instead of sending cold requests, she suggests commenting on a prospect's posts for a week with meaningful insights. Engaging in the same circles they do and following them on other platforms like X or YouTube also helps. This approach creates a visibility circle that turns a stranger into a recognized name, which significantly increases the chances of a successful connection.
