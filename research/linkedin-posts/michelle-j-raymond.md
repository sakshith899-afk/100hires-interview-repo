Brief: Michelle explains a recent update from LinkedIn's engineering team about how the feed works now. The algorithm does not simply show your posts to your connections. It uses AI to understand the specific topic of the post and shares it with people interested in that topic, even if they do not follow you. Her main advice is to avoid posting random content. Instead, choose 1 to 3 main topics. You must ensure your company page, profile, and posts are all consistent so the AI knows who to show your content to. You do not need to post more often; you just need to be very clear about what you do.
