Research Sources: B2B SaaS LinkedIn Organic Strategy

This document outlines the 10 B2B SaaS marketing operators and founders selected for this research project. They were specifically chosen because they share actionable frameworks for founder-led growth, company page distribution, and B2B organic content. These are systems that are highly relevant to an ATS startup like 100Hires targeting lean HR teams and founders.

Focus: B2B SaaS Video and Organic Content
Why Selected: He shares highly tactical frameworks on how to take "boring" B2B software concepts and turn them into highly engaging, shareable organic content that drives inbound demo requests.
